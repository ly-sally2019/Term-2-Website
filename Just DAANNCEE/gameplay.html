Gameplay

Just Dance is a very simple game that even kids can enjoy. It has been designed for parties, families, or even people who just want to exercise. In this game, you just follow along with the dancer on the screen with a controller to monitor your movements. The objective of the game is to score the most points for your dancing and earn the most stars by following the dance moves correctly. Just Dance explains the game very simply, saying that to play, you must mimic the person on the screen, as if they were the "mirror-image" of yourself. To start, just select your type of game style(party or workout), select your song or workout course, and dance!
